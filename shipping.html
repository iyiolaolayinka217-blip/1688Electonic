Shipping FAQs

When will my order ship?

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Express orders receive priority processing and typically ship within 24 hours.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on orders over ¥3500. Free shipping applies to Zone 1-3 destinations.

Can I change my shipping address?

Address changes can be made within 24 hours of placing your order. Contact our support team immediately for assistance.
