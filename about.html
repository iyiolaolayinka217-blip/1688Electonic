About 1688 Electronic Mart

Your trusted partner for wholesale electronics since 2010

Our Story

1688 Electronic Mart was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to make high-quality electronics accessible to businesses worldwide at wholesale prices.

What started as a small trading company in Shenzhen has grown into one of Asia's leading B2B electronics marketplaces, connecting manufacturers directly with retailers, resellers, and enterprise buyers.

Today, we serve over 50,000 business customers across 120+ countries, offering everything from consumer electronics to industrial components.
