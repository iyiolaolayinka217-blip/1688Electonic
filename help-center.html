Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting our Track Order page and entering your order number. You can also find tracking information in your order confirmation email or by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. Products must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Some items like software and consumables may not be eligible for return. Visit our Returns page for complete details.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by destination and method. Standard shipping typically takes 7-14 business days internationally. Express shipping delivers in 3-7 business days. For detailed shipping information by region, visit our Shipping Info page.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We specialize in wholesale and offer tiered pricing based on order quantity. The more you buy, the more you save. For large volume orders (100+ units), please contact our sales team at sales@1688mart.com for custom quotes.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Click on "Register" in the top right corner of any page, fill in your details, and verify your email address. Business accounts may require additional documentation for wholesale pricing access.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers for large orders, and Alibaba Trade Assurance. All payments are processed securely with SSL encryption.
